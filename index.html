Hit Reminder

This App let you to create a reminder of any event,occasions or Birthdays

How Does it Work ?



Simply Sign Up with your valid Email Address and Mobile No, After that you will be able to create a reminder, the reminder are send via Email and SMS.

In Email you get a more detailed reminder message, while in SMS just a small note

How to set a Reminder



After Signing Up, Select the date of the Event, and then select the date after which we will start reminding you about the event, and then enter the type and message for the reminder,

You will get Email and SMS on daily basis after the date that you have set to "Start Reminding After" and at the end you will get a "Final Reminder" on the event date
